# Contributor Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in
this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body
size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender
identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status,
nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and
orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, insulting or derogatory comments,
personal or political attacks, and publishing others' private information.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be
reported to the maintainer at `ferdakerim@gmail.com`. All complaints will be
reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/),
version 2.1.
